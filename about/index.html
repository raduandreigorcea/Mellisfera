Our Heritage

What started with five wooden hives in a meadow behind our farmhouse has grown into a thriving family business spanning three generations.

Robert Mellisfera's passion for beekeeping began as a weekend hobby but quickly became a calling. His deep respect for bees and commitment to natural methods laid the foundation for everything we do today.

Today, we maintain over 200 hives across 50 acres of pristine countryside, but our core values remain unchanged: bee welfare first, pure natural honey, and sustainable practices that protect the environment.
